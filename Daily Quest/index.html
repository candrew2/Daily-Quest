Turn Everyday Tasks into Achievements

Daily Quest is a motivational habit-tracking app that transforms your daily routine into a game. Build habits, prove progress, and unlock fun rewards.

What is Daily Quest?

Daily Quest helps students and young adults stay motivated by turning everyday tasks into achievements. Create task lists across daily, every-other-day, and weekly habits, or track important one-time events like exams and assignments.

When you complete a task, submit a photo as proof to earn points — then spend those points in the Arcade to unlock and progress through fun mini-games.
